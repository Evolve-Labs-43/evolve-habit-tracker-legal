1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Ritualist (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application Ritualist and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

Ritualist is a productivity and habit-tracking application. It helps you build routines, track completions, and view progress metrics. Ritualist is not a medical device and does not provide medical, psychiatric, psychological, or therapeutic services (see Medical disclaimer).

By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

2. Who may use Ritualist (age restrictions)

COPPA / children’s privacy The Service is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us at support@ritualistsystem.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

Parents and guardians are responsible for supervising minors’ use of devices and accounts.

6. Data retention

  • Account and habit data: Retained while your account is active.
  • After account deletion: We delete your Firebase Authentication account and Firestore user data (including synced habits under your user path) when you use in-app deletion or confirm an email deletion request.
  • Analytics / Crashlytics: Retained per Google Firebase default settings; we configure retention where the console allows shorter periods.
  • Purchases: Google Play and RevenueCat retain records per their policies for billing, fraud prevention, and tax.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit (TLS) for communications with Firebase and other cloud services, Firebase Authentication, and access controls on production systems.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use a strong password and protect your device.

To manage or cancel subscriptions, use Google Play → Payments & subscriptions.

9. Data deletion instructions

How to delete your account and associated data You can request complete deletion of your Ritualist account and cloud-synced data using either method below.

Option A — Delete inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Ritualist and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → your Profile / Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

This permanently deletes your Firebase Authentication account and associated Firestore data (habits, sections, settings, and synced documents), and cancels local reminders tied to your habits. You may be asked to sign in again recently before deletion for security (Firebase requirement).

Option B — Email deletion request

If you cannot access the app, you may request complete data deletion by emailing:

support@ritualistsystem.com

Use the email subject line: Data Deletion Request

Please include:

  • The email address linked to your Ritualist account
  • A brief statement that you want all account and associated data permanently deleted

We will verify ownership of the account and complete deletion within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days), unless a longer period is required by law. We may retain minimal information only where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.
